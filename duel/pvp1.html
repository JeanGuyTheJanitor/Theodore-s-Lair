Bunny Walk

It can be dangerous to measure this against the shooter when you are a warrior. Thanks to a mechanics of the game it is possible to kill the attacker without getting hit.

In Diablo 1 there is an invisible grid on which everything moves. this grid is invisible. in this way it is possible to pass in the trajectory of a projectile without being hit while passing diagonally.

in this way a player can throw diagonally then towards his attacker. Once the shooter hits he will be stun locked and will no longer be able to move or shoot.

the South walk
