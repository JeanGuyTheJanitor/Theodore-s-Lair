Theodore's Lair is a new Diablo 1 DevilutionX community where you can meet new players and trade your items with others.

-2023-02-18 update of the template of the website

-2023-02-18 new calculator in the info section

DevilutionX is a port of Diablo and Hellfire that strives to make it simple to run the game while providing engine improvements, bugfixes, and some optional quality of life features.

We hope to provide a good starting point for mods, in addition to the full Devilution source code we also provide modding tools.
